About

Hello I'm Jigdel Kuyee. I maintain Tibet Data , work in the power engineering sector and enjoy being outdoors. My work primarily revolves around working with utilities to develop their asset management strategies. Our electricity infrastructure was built mostly after the Second World War and their replacement time has now arrived. Using a combination of the asset's age and condition data, we are able to identify and prioritize the replacement of these assets. I am also leading the management and sales of the e-WorkSAFE tool - an online safety tool that helps electricians follow the CSA Z462 standards to operate their electrical equipments safely.

I also have a keen interest in Data Science. I enjoy exploring datasets, analyzing them and visualising any hidden value within them - some of these projects are listed below. My team won Canada's biggest hackathon where we used the government's open datasets on career projections and job demands to build a career path search guide. Also, I have been lucky enough to co-author an IEEE paper. I enjoy volunteering when I can and have previously been on the board of Tibetan Canadian Cultural Centre and worked with Kids Internet Safety Alliance as part of Endeavor Volunteer Consulting for Non-Profits.
