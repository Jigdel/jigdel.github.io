About

Hello, Jigdel here. I have built a small software company that focuses on building and managing custom software products. In addition, we also operate two small software products (SaaS). I used to work in the electricity sector as both a utility engineer and later as a lead consultant.

For hobbies, other than going on hikes/treks, I enjoy exploring datasets, analyzing them and visualising any hidden value within them - some of these projects are listed below. I also maintain Tibet Data and my team luckily won Canada's biggest hackathon where we used the government's open datasets on career projections and job demands to build a career path search guide. Also, I have been fortunate enough to co-author an IEEE paper. I enjoy volunteering when I can and have previously been on the board of Tibetan Canadian Cultural Centre and worked with Kids Internet Safety Alliance as part of Endeavor Volunteer Consulting for Non-Profits.
